Meanderthal / Oaks Hikes

This is the new website for displaying information on trips taken by the Meanderthals and now the (primarily) Oaks hiking group. A new website was required because Google is dropping support for the old ("legacy") Google Sites which has been used up to now for that purpose.

The maps associated with each hike are based on Google My Maps. Photos are stored in Google Photos and organized into Google Photo Albums. All 'links' have been checked, but let me know if you find any problems that may have been overlooked!

Notes:

1) In some cases a hike was not correctly saved when the hike ended. In those cases, the recorded Statistics were incorrect and therefore the Statistics links for those hikes have been removed. The Map and Profile information should be correct except (possibly) for the part after the actual end of the hike.

2) For some hikes, reason unknown, the 'max' and 'avg' values for Speed and Pace are reported as being the same. This anomalous - and obviously incorrect - result is a function of the program used to record hikes; I don't know which, if either, of the displayed values is correct! This problem has been corrected in more recent hikes and the 'max' and 'avg' values should now be correctly displayed.

3) The Max speed: and Fastest pace: entries in the Statistics section do not have any real significance - they are just a couple of results reported by the program used to record the hikes. THESE VALUES ARE NO LONGER BEING RECORDED OR DISPLAYED!

Wayne
